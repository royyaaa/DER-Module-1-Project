## Introduction
As we all know, the brain is one of the most important parts of the human body. Therefore, maintaining brain health is essential. However, there are many factors that can cause damage or a decline in brain function, which are often overlooked. Recently, a new term has emerged to describe cognitive decline: “brainrot”.

Brainrot refers to a decline in cognitive, intellectual, and mental functioning caused by excessive consumption of low-quality, short-form content commonly found on social media, as well as trivial and unchallenging content. This phenomenon is widely experienced by adolescents, young adults, and even children and adults who spend a significant amount of time on screen-based activities.

Due to this phenomenon, many people have become interested in studying it and exploring ways to prevent or overcome brainrot. Increasing awareness has also encouraged individuals to share knowledge through content they create and upload to various platforms. One of the most popular platforms for accessing information is YouTube, which has a large user base compared to other social media platforms. YouTube offers a wide variety of content that can be tailored to users’ needs and interests.
